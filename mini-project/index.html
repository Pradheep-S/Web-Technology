Benefits of Eye Donation:

  • Restore vision and transform lives.
  • Help someone see the world again.
  • Leave a lasting legacy of kindness and generosity.
  • Bring hope to individuals suffering from visual impairment.
  • Contribute to scientific research and medical advancements.

Eye Donation Statistics:

Did you know that:

  • More than 10 million people worldwide suffer from corneal blindness.
  • Every 5 seconds, someone in the world goes blind, but 80% of blindness is preventable or treatable.
  • One eye donor can potentially restore vision for up to two individuals.
  • Eye donation is a simple process that does not disfigure the donor.
